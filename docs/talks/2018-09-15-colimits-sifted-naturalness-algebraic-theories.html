Of Colimits, Sifted and Not, and the Naturalness of Algebraic Theories

Date: September 15, 2018
Last modified: October 22, 2025
Venue: Slow‑pitch Seminar, University of Colorado, Boulder

Abstract

This talk explores colimits (both sifted and not) and the naturalness of algebraic theories, providing insights into the structure of algebraic categories.
