We Must Add Structure to Deep Learning Because... Revisited

July 16, 2025, Posted by Paul Lessard

IS THE WATCH SERVER WORKING? Back in the heady days of March 2023 I did an interview with Tim & Keith of Machine Learning Street Talk. In the year and half since I’ve learned a great deal; about machine learning, about business, and about antibiotics as rosacea treatment - you’ll see that I appear healthier in the second video.

I did another very impromptu chat with Tim and Keith in June of this year, the last time I visited Symbolica’s London office.

While I think my outfit and hair are better in this second talk, the interview was far more all over the place. So I figured I’d give a shot at collecting the sensibility I’ve come to after a year and half of working as a category theorist in deep learning in an industrial as opposed to academic context.
