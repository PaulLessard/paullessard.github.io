## Notes

This talk was delivered as part of the Pure Math Seminar series at UNSW. In the talk I explained how, informed by Homotopy Type Theory, one can "read" Cisisnki's presheaves as models of homotopy types as presentations of (\infty,1) categories of homotopy coherent limit theories.
